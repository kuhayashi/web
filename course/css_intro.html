Style Placement

Inline Styling

Just insere a "style" attribute to the element that you want to add style. e.g. <p style="text-align: center;">
However, this is the least used way of styling because of its unefficiency.

Link to a separate stylesheet

In most cases, we prefer to have a separate stylesheet, usually called "style.css" and link that sheet to our pages to ensure the consistency of every page.
To do this, we add a "link" tag in the <head>, with an attribute "rel"="stylesheet" and a href attribute of its location. e.g. <link rel="stylesheet" href="style.css">

Head Styles

In cases of head styles, they are usually used to override external stylesheet.
